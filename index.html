How It Works

Revolutionary Growing Technology

Our patented loop system maximises growing area within minimal floor space, enabling industrial-scale production of high-value crops.

Moving Conveyor Belt

The game changer: plants travel on a continuously moving belt through different growth zones. Constant motion triggers gravitropism — producing higher biomass, richer active ingredients and up to 120x more yield on less space.

100% Soilless & Clean

Aeroponic misting with zero soil, zero substrate, zero contamination. 95% less water and pharma-grade purity — enabling complete biomass utilisation from roots to leaves.

Lowest CAPEX & OPEX

80% lower CAPEX than conventional indoor farming. Minimal energy consumption, low labour costs, and maximum automation — the most cost-efficient vertical farming system on the market.

Modular & Scalable

Start with a single module, scale to industrial farms. Each module operates independently — grow different crops simultaneously. Worldwide IP secured.

Why OrbiFarm

Built for Profitability

A complete platform that combines hardware, software and bioprocessing to deliver unmatched unit economics.

Factory, Not Farm

Designed as a continuous industrial process with conveyor logic, automation and QA gates — not a greenhouse with sensors.

Superior ROI

~115% ROI in ~10 months for medicinal plants, ~51% ROI in ~23 months for protein crops. Short payback periods and de-risked economics.

Up to 120x Yield

Up to 120x higher yield per m² vs. outdoor cultivation through full-plant utilisation and year-round multi-harvest cycles.

Recipe-Driven Production

Standardised grow recipes tuned for actives, purity and particle properties — specification-driven output at pharma grade.

Digital Twin

Process digitalisation with simulation, optimisation and predictive maintenance across the entire installed base.

Strategic Partners

Backed by Fraunhofer (R&D) and Flottweg (downstream processing) — a strong ecosystem enabling rapid industrial scale-up.

100% Clean Cultivation

No soil, no contamination — fully soilless aeroponics ensures pharma-grade purity. Critical for medicinal plants and regulated markets.

Whole Plant Utilisation

Leaves, stems and roots across multiple SKUs and sidestreams. Zero waste, maximum value extraction from every harvest.

Gravitropism Effect

Continuous rotation triggers natural gravitropism — boosting biomass growth, enhancing active ingredient concentration and increasing overall yield.
